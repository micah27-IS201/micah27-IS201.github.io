My Love for Soccer ⚽

Raised in Madrid. Raised on the game.

My Story

Growing up in Madrid, soccer was more than just a hobby; rather, it was what connected me to everyone else. It was how I made friends, learned the language, and it completely helped mold me into the person I am today.

Why I Love Soccer

  1. The Fundamentals
    • Running with the ball at my feet
    • Scoring an important goal
    • Building chemistry with my teammates and helping each other play at a high level
  2. The Culture
    • The feeling inside a stadium when a goal is scored
    • The palpable energy on matchday
    • Singing in the Bernabeu stands
  3. The History
    • Witnessing an important part of sport history
    • Champions League and World Cup games
    • Seeing Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi play against each other

Real Madrid — My Club

Real Madrid has always been special to me. Even before I lived there, I was familiar with the club, the players, the history, and the culture surrounding the association. Being able to actually live there meant I could interact with fans who felt the same way as I did about it. It was really neat.

My Favorite Player: Marcelo

One of my favorite players to ever play for the team is Marcelo. He has one of the best first touches in soccer history and watching him play with Ronaldo on a weekly basis was awesome. I miss having him at the club and love going back and watching old highlights from when he was there.

My Favorite Real Madrid Moment

This video shows Cristiano Ronaldo scoring a bicycle kick in a high-stakes quarterfinal of the Champions League. Everything about his technique is incredible. I remember watching it live and how it blew my mind then. It still does, to this day.
