Real Madrid — My Club

Real Madrid has always been special to me. Even before living there, I followed the club, its history, players, and incredible fan culture. Living in Madrid allowed me to experience the atmosphere of the city and the passion of the supporters firsthand.

My Favorite Player: Marcelo

Marcelo was one of the most entertaining players to ever wear the Real Madrid jersey. His creativity, technique, and partnership with Cristiano Ronaldo made the left side of the field incredibly dangerous. Watching him play live was unforgettable.

My Favorite Real Madrid Moment

Cristiano Ronaldo’s bicycle kick against Juventus in the Champions League is one of the greatest goals ever scored. The technique, timing, and difficulty make it unforgettable.
